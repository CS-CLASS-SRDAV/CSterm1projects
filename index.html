A Glimpse Into

Python Projects
for SDG Goals

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.

The Term-1 Project for the CS class of XI-C 2021-22 was to prepare a Python program on the SDGs that will impact the society in a positive way. After brainstorming and deliberation, the students rose up to the challenge.

Showcased here are the culmination of their efforts.

These are the Term 1 end projects of the CS students of class XI-C, assigned to us for our assessment.
SDGs (or Sustainable Development Goals), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.
This specefic theme was given to us by our mentor teacher, to instill a sense of the international scale and sensetivity towards global issues to our work.
I think this enabled students to think outside the box, and at the same time, sensitize themselves towards problems from a global perspective.
Our syllabus for the term 1 in academic session 2021-22 in CS was mostly Python. Hence, most of these projects include python as their core.
